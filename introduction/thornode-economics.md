How to get rewards?

At the 15th of each month, we will add the earnings of that month to the reward pools. Rewards from the previous month will be paid by airdrop. All you need to do is holding your pass in your wallet. In Thor Labs discord server, [#rewards](https://discord.com/channels/985679905868115988/1054356660753281096/1069004755495551016) channel shows your holdings and points for the current month.\
Snapshots are taken randomly. Rewards are distributed as a proportion of the total points. For each pass you hold grants you 1 point for each snapshot.
